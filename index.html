About OMARCH

Where Vision Becomes Architecture

OMARCH Architects & Associates is Delhi's distinguished architecture and interior design firm, renowned for creating elegant, classy, and aesthetic spaces that transcend conventional design boundaries. Based in Lajpat Nagar, we serve discerning clients across Delhi NCR with exceptional architectural solutions.

Our philosophy centers on the belief that architecture should be both visually stunning and functionally superior. We specialize in residential villas, luxury homes, commercial spaces, retail interiors, and bespoke design solutions that reflect sophistication and timeless beauty.

With over 112 satisfied clients and a perfect 4.9-star rating, OMARCH has established itself as the trusted choice for those seeking premium architectural and interior design services in Delhi. Every project is a testament to our commitment to excellence, precision, and aesthetic perfection.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our architectural and interior design services.

What services does OMARCH provide?
OMARCH offers comprehensive architectural and interior design services including residential architecture (villas, luxury homes, penthouses), commercial architecture (offices, retail spaces), luxury interior design, jewellery store design, fashion boutique design, 3D visualization, and complete project management from concept to completion.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Design development typically takes 3-6 weeks, approvals 2-3 weeks, and construction 4-12 months depending on project size. We provide detailed timelines during initial consultation and maintain regular communication throughout the project.
What areas do you serve?
OMARCH serves clients throughout Delhi NCR including Lajpat Nagar, South Delhi, Greater Kailash, Defence Colony, Noida, Gurgaon, and surrounding areas. Our office is conveniently located near Mool Chand Metro Station in Vikram Vihar, Lajpat Nagar IV.
Do you provide 3D visualization services?
Yes! We create detailed 3D renderings and walkthroughs for all our projects. This allows clients to visualize their spaces before construction begins, ensuring complete satisfaction with design decisions regarding layouts, materials, colors, and finishes.
What is the cost of your architectural services?
Our fees are customized based on project scope, complexity, and services required. Architectural design fees typically range from 6-12% of construction cost. Interior design pricing varies based on area and specifications. We offer transparent pricing and provide detailed quotations during free consultation.
Do you handle project approvals and permissions?
Absolutely! We manage all aspects of building approvals including municipal permissions, structural clearances, NOCs, and regulatory compliance. Our team has extensive experience working with Delhi development authorities ensuring smooth approval processes.
Can you work within my budget?
Yes, we work with various budget ranges and offer tailored solutions. During consultation, we discuss your requirements and budget to create designs that maximize value while maintaining our standards of elegance and quality. We provide transparent cost breakdowns and help optimize choices.
What makes OMARCH different from other architects?
OMARCH is distinguished by our philosophy of "elegant, classy & aesthetic" design. With a 4.9-star rating from 112+ clients, we combine sophisticated aesthetics with functional excellence. Our personalized approach, attention to detail, and commitment to creating timeless spaces sets us apart in Delhi's architectural landscape.
